How to Get There

📍 Location

The apartment is located in the Beaupré Residence, just a few meters from the beach, at Canet-en-Roussillon.

🚗 By Car

Canet-en-Roussillon is easily accessible by car. From the A9 motorway (La Languedocienne), take exit 41 – Perpignan Nord, then follow the signs for Canet-en-Roussillon / Canet Plage.

  • Perpignan → 15 minutes
  • Montpellier → 1h30
  • Toulouse → 2h30
  • Barcelona → 2h15

🚆 By Train

The nearest station is Perpignan Train Station, served by:

  • TGV Paris → Perpignan (approx. 5h)
  • TGV Lyon → Perpignan (approx. 3h30)
  • Regional TER trains from Narbonne, Béziers, Montpellier…

From Perpignan station, you can reach Canet:

  • By bus (line 6) → around 30 minutes
  • By taxi → around 20 minutes
  • By private transfer (VTC) → available on request

🚌 By Bus

The Sankéo network connects Perpignan to Canet-en-Roussillon.

  • Line 6: Perpignan → Canet Plage (stop near the residence)
  • Closest stop: Terrasses du Levant
  • Frequency: every 20–30 minutes depending on the season

✈️ By Plane

The nearest airport is:

  • Perpignan – Rivesaltes Airport (PGF) → approx. 20 minutes

Other accessible airports:

  • Carcassonne → approx. 1h15
  • Montpellier → approx. 1h40
  • Barcelona El Prat → approx. 2h15

🚲 By Bike or On Foot

The residence is located in the heart of Canet Plage, with many cycle paths and pedestrian promenades. Everything is within walking distance: beach, shops, restaurants, and activities.
