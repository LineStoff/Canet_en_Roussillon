📝 Description

Located in the Beaupré Residence, our 45 m² apartment offers the perfect setting for a seaside holiday. You are just 1 minute from the warm sand, with no road to cross — a true luxury for enjoying the Mediterranean safely and peacefully.

The apartment features an exceptional 45 m² terrace with sea view, ideal for outdoor meals, relaxing moments, or summer evenings. A garden within the residence adds greenery and calm.
