🎡 Must‑Do Activities

Oniria Aquarium

An immersive and spectacular journey through the oceans. Perfect for families and sea lovers.

Visit Website

Port of Canet-en-Roussillon

A lively marina, ideal for strolling, admiring the boats, enjoying waterfront restaurants, or taking a sea excursion. A pleasant atmosphere both day and night.

Visit Website

Luna Park Argelès-sur-Mer

The largest amusement park in the region, open all summer. Thrill rides, Ferris wheel, game stands, festive and nighttime atmosphere — a must‑visit for families and fun‑seekers. Only 20 minutes from Canet.

Visit Website

Playground

A boat‑shaped playground facing the sea, accessible from age 2. The area is secure but remains under parental supervision.

🧺 Markets

Canet-en-Roussillon

A typical and lively market where you’ll find local products, fresh fish and Catalan specialties in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Visit website

Argelès-sur-Mer

The Argelès-sur-Mer market is one of the largest and most vibrant on the coast. You’ll find local producers, colorful stalls, Catalan specialties, crafts and a very summery, friendly atmosphere. Perfect for strolling with family and discovering the flavors of the region.

Visit website

Barcarès

The Barcarès market is appreciated for its friendly atmosphere and its many varied stalls. Between local products, fresh fish, crafts and Mediterranean specialties, it’s an ideal place to enjoy the seaside atmosphere and take a pleasant walk.
